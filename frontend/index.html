Ask budget-policy questions and review confidence.

Scope note

This Q&A system is not an official government FAQ.

Data source: Answers are generated only from Singapore Budget Statements, Annex and Round-Up Speeches from FY2016–FY2025 within the project’s document scope.

Bot accuracy: As an automated system, this tool may misinterpret or miss relevant information; users should confirm details directly from the cited sources.

Not an eligibility checker: This system does not determine individual eligibility or payout amounts and does not replace official eligibility tools.

Retrieval & citation, not analysis: The system’s role is to retrieve, summarize, and cite relevant budget policy documents based on the query. It does not perform financial planning, legal interpretation, or personalized policy analysis.

Encourage primary-source due diligence: Users are encouraged to review the cited evidence or alternatives directly for full context and authoritative details.

It does not reference other government websites, agencies, or eligibility calculators.
